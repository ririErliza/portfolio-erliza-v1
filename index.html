About me

My name is Erliza Erbaryanti, I love developing websites and I love the role of a web developer. For me, web developer is like a bridge between two parties with common ideas or purpose or goal, and it gives me a sense of fulfillment if I can connect people to work together to create sustainable improvements for others. As much as I love what I am doing, I also like to keep a balance life. So when I am not coding or learning, during weekend I go out with my small family, enjoying nature, hiking, or simply wandering in forest.

Speaking about my background in a brief, I graduated from Metallurgy and Materials Engineering. Being an Engineer was an exciting phase for me, however after some years being absent from the Industry, my life evolved. Then, there comes the period of questioning myself "What do I really want to do for the rest of my life? Something that sparks joy but also matching to my life goal?"

After some thinking and researching, finally I decided to make a career change into Web Development. At first I did self-study but after joining a coding school in 2021, with good guidance I progressed much better. In 2022 I completed my training in Web Development, and to this time I keep working to improve my skills while enjoying my new role as a web developer.
